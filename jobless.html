The Case

Jobless is a future job searching platform and freelance marketplace made by a group of students from Oslo Norway. The website is amied at Scadinavian Freelancers in order to make it easier for them to find work. they also have the possibility to publish their own services that other users can buy on the marketplace.

We also want to make it easier for the avrage joe in the street to buy a service from a freelancer. Need a webpage, custome music beat, logo, or new graphichs to your t-shirt that you sell, and want to use local freelancer ? Hopefully in not long you can get your product done via Jobless.
