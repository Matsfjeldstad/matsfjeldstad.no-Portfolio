The Case

This project was the semester project the first semester I had at Noroff. We were going to show our abilitys in using Figma, Html and Css. We were going to make a museum site with Its core target audience is primary and middle school children (7-15) and families with young children. It also stated that the website should be informative and appealing, that will attract both middle school pupils and their parents.

This page is pure HTML and CSS, with no Javascript involved. this was one of my first times implementing css animation, but I think it worked out fine. All images is taken from pexel.com, Unslash.com, and pixabay, and later edited in photoshop to get desired effect and style.
